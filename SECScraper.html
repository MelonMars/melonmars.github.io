SEC Scraper

In the US, the SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission), puts almost all publicly available filings onto their EDGAR database. This includes form 13F, which organizations or people with over $100 Million worth of assets must file, listing what assets they have.

The database is set up in a very easily scraped way. This allowed me to write a scraper, and then graph it all as a network graph, and compute the properties of the stock market.
