Fake News Generator

This is a fake news generator, that makes up headlines and text, fully autonomously, based off of what it scrapes from other news sites.

This was created in the summer of 2023, and was before I had heard of any other fake news generators, making this possibly one of the first ones.

It is only usable via the terminal, and is designed to work with the Wizard LLM, I believe specifically Wizard Vicuna 7B, but I could be wrong (It's been a year).
