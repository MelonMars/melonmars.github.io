Avionics PCB

This is a printed circuit board, that is based around a barometer and a 3-axis accelerometer to function as sort of backup avionics for a plane.

The accelerometer allows for a turn coordinator, ground speed indicator, and attitude indicator.

The barometer helps as an altitude indicator.

All of this is outputted onto a display of LEDs.

It is also all driven by an ATMega (I think an ATMega382).
