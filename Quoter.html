Fake News Generator

Quoter

This is a bot that creates fake quotes from a webui.

It has a high temperature setting, so a high randomness, and then it will create a random quote in the format: "[Background Image] Quote"

Then, the [Background Image] is removed, and is replaced by a stable diffusion created background image, while the Quoter has a "- NAME" appended to it, based on a randomly selected name.

The quote is then added on top of the Background Image using Python Pillow.

I would say it is responsible for a lot of funny fake, misattributed quotes.
